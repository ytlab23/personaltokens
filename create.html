Your token will be named according to the pattern: "first name last name", e.g. "John Smith".

Last name must be at least one character long and cannot contain any special characters or numbers.

Add a symbol of your token that will be part of your token’s domain. e.g. ”personaltokens.io/JS”.
Your token will be worth more if it's trustworthy, give your real first and last name!
Remember that token name can’t be changed in the future.
